üö¶ Project Overview

The Virginia Transportation Safety Index (VTSI) project develops a dynamic, data-driven safety scoring system for intersections and roadway segments across Virginia. We combine crash data, traffic volumes, and severity weighting to provide real-time, actionable insights for improving roadway safety.

Traffic Intersection

Our scoring focuses on intersection-level risk, leveraging temporal patterns (e.g., peak hours), geometric context, approach speeds, and multimodal activity to surface locations needing near-term attention.

Roadway Segments

Segment scores normalize crash frequency by exposure (AADT) and weight by severity to identify corridors with disproportionate safety risk.
